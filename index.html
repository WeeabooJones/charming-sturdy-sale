Tips For Staying Healthy

  • Stay at home as much as possible.
  • Only go out if you REALLY need to.
  • Always wear an anti-bacterial mask and gloves when going outside.
  • If youd don't wear gloves, then be sure to bring a small bottle of hand sanitizer.
  • Wash your hands often.
  • Thoroughly cook any meats and eggs.
  • Disinfect surfaces in your home.
  • Avoid any large crowds.
  • Practice social distancing.
  • Always cover your mouth/nose when coughing/sneezing.

Remember to stay safe and stay healthy everyone! Best of luck to you all!
